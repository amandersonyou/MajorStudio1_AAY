Landscape Generator

Unique collages are built from the Metropolitan Museum of Art's collection of thirty open access landscape paintings. They are divided into equal slivers and re-aligned at random, oriented vertically based on an assigned horizon value between 0 and 1. The new resulting landscape shows a steady horizon across the middle, demonstrating variation in sky-to-land ratios throughout the original works.

Refresh the window for endless options. Select a sliver to learn more about a particular painting.
